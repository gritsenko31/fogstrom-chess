🌫️ Fog of War Chess — How to Play

🎯 Goal
Capture the enemy King to win. All standard chess piece movements apply.
🌫️ Fog of War
You only see squares your pieces currently occupy or can legally move to. Everything else is hidden.
👁️ Enemy Pieces
Enemy pieces are invisible until they step into one of your visible squares. Expect the unexpected!
🤖 AI Opponent
You play White, the AI plays Black. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty before starting.
♟ Special Moves
Castling, en passant, and pawn promotion (auto-queen at rank 8) are fully supported.
💡 Strategy Tip
Control the center early — more pieces = more vision. A hidden enemy can attack from the fog!
